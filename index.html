Halo Energy Sword

Full Size Image

This is a replica of an energy sword from the Halo franchise made from Mega Construx building blocks. I built this replica in six hours after buying the set from Target. It was a bit annoying with the massive instructions falling off the tiny table I used, putting together the 500+ pieces it was made of, etc., but I am pleased with the result.

Snap Game

Game

In this Snap game, the goal is to pop as many balloons as possible. When you pop a certain number of balloons, a floating eye pops up and starts trying to kill you. Every time your score goes up, the balloons get slightly smaller and slightly faster.

Makecode DOOM Genius Hour Project

The Program

This is one of my Genius Hour projects from the 2020-2021 school year. It’s a program made for a Micro-Bit which, when loaded onto the Micro-Bit, will allow it to play E1M1-At DOOM’s Gate from the hit 1993 game DOOM. One of the main struggles of this project was getting all of the notes correct.
